Erquan Yingyue-Abing Introduction

}

Free Erquan Yingyue piano sheet music is provided for you. So if you like it, just download it here. Enjoy It!

Erquan Yingyue is a Chinese piece played by play slightly larger erhu. It was created by blind Chinese musician Abing.

Erquan Yingyue is the most famous piece for Abing. which is named after a spring in Wuxi (which is today part of Xihui Park). It is still played as a standard erhu piece, although it necessitates a special set of strings that are tuned lower than normal erhu strings.
