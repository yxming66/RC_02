Flower Dance is a song that was performed by Japanese Dj Okawari. Okawari came from Shizuoka, the genre-based jazz-hiphop and chill-hop. In 2009, he shows us another masterpiece-Mirror, it is a a suffocating gorgeous made by three perfect combination-piano electronic drum female voice. And we might say that Flower Dance is one of his representative works.
